Booking Procedures
1. Priority will be given to programmes organized by the university.
2. Full payment must be made before the programme started. Booking will automatically cancel if the payment not has been made.
3. Any cancellation of programme should be acknowledged to the Sports Development Centre seven(7) days prior to the actual date.
4. SDC will bot be responsible for any injury / accident or lost of personal belongings before, during and after programmes.
5. User will be responsible for any lost and damage of the facilities.
6. Only pack food is allowed in sport complex. Always keep clean and litter in the bins provided.
7. Users are requested to follow the rules and regulations and IIUM dress code set by SDC.
8. SDC has the right to cancel the approval to use the facilities at anytime if the users failed to follow the rules and regulations of sports complex.
